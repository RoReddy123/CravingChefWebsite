Tomato Bruschetta
✕
⏱ 15 min
👤 4
★ Easy
Ingredients
6 slices rustic bread
4 ripe tomatoes, diced
2 cloves garlic
Fresh basil leaves
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Salt & black pepper
Steps
1
Toast bread slices until golden and crisp on both sides.
2
Rub each slice with a halved garlic clove while warm.
3
Mix diced tomatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper and basil.
4
Spoon mixture over toast and serve immediately.
